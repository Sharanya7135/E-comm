Welcome to our newly launched website

Established on September 9, 2002, Computer Solutions Ind Pvt. Ltd. has been a trusted name in the industry for over two decades. From its inception, the company has been committed to excellence, growing steadily to become a privately registered (ROC) limited company on the same founding date.

Our Leadership

At the helm of our company are two visionary directors who have guided us through an incredible journey of innovation and success. Their expertise and leadership continue to drive our mission forward.

Our Team

With a strong workforce of 20+ highly qualified professionals and employees boasting 30+ years of industry experience, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional services and solutions.

Our Presence

Headquartered in Mumbai, we have expanded our reach with branches in Bangalore, Mangalore, and Hyderabad, ensuring a robust network to serve our clients efficiently across multiple locations.

We Are Authorized Dealers For:

We take pride in being authorized dealers for a range of high-quality products from leading brands:

  • HP Laser Printers, HP Deskjet Printers, HP Scanners, HP Consumables
  • Epson Dot Matrix Printers
  • Cisco & Linksys Products
  • Seagate Products (Internal & External Hard Disks)
  • Samsung Monitors, Hard Disks & DVD Writers
  • Lipi Products
  • Intel Products
  • Kingston Products
  • Dell Products
  • Lenovo Laptops, Desktops & Accessories
  • Western Digital SSD & NAS Box

Authorized Service Center

As a policy, Computer Solutions (I) Pvt. Ltd. has always emphasized providing strong after-sales support and service to its clients.

Service Centre Activities

Computer Solutions (I) Pvt. Ltd. is the authorized service center for the entire range of HP Deskjet, LaserJet Printers, and computer hardware.

Service Centre Offers:

  • Repairs and servicing of printers under warranty
  • Post-warranty repairs of printers on a chargeable basis
  • Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for printers
  • Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for computer systems

We offer a special ON-SITE warranty ranging from 1 to 3 years on these products. Our dedicated team of highly qualified and experienced engineers ensures efficient services.

We proudly serve over 1000 reputed clients across India, including multinational corporations, private sector firms, government organizations, and large businesses. Additionally, we cater to more than 10,000 individual customerswith our top-notch services.
