Help Page

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I place an order?

A: To place an order, browse our catalog, select the laptop you want, and click 'Add to Cart'. Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

Q: What is the return policy?

A: We offer a 30-day return policy for all laptops. Please ensure the product is in its original condition and packaging.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use this number on our website to track your order.

Payments

We accept payment via cash on delivery. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

If you encounter any issues during payment, please contact our support team for assistance.

Chatbot Assistance

Our chatbot is available 24/7 to assist you with any queries. Simply click on the chatbot icon at the bottom right corner of the screen to start a conversation.

The chatbot can help with order tracking, product information, and general inquiries.
